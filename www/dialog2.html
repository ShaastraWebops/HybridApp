Beyond Rupees: My Date with Design
Date and Time : 25th Jan, 11:00am-12:30pm
Venue : ICSR Auditorium
Bio : Dr. D. Udaya Kumar is an Assistant Professor at Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, India. He is the designer of the Indian Rupee Symbol. He has a PhD and Master’s degree in Design (Visual communication) from Industrial Design Centre, IIT Bombay. He completed his Bachelor’s degree in Architecture from School of Architecture and Planning, Anna University, Chennai.
